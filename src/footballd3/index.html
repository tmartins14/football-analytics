Progressive Map — UEFA Euro 2024 Final

All passes & carries · progressive actions highlighted · 25%-of-remaining-distance-to-goal rule · set pieces excluded

Shows all of a team's open-play passes and carries. Color encodes action type for every action — red for passes, navy for carries. Progressive actions (those that moved the ball at least 25% of the remaining distance to goal) are bold with arrowheads; non-progressive actions are thin and faded. Switch the filter to progressive-only to remove the background layer. Passes show completed and incomplete attempts; carries are completed only (StatsBomb does not model incomplete carries).
